**ğŸ“Š Business Questions Answered**

The following key business questions were answered using SQL:

1. What is the total revenue generated by male vs female customers?  
2. Which customers used discounts but still spent more than the average purchase amount?  
3. Which are the top 5 products with the highest average review ratings?  
4. How does the average purchase amount compare between Standard and Express shipping?  
5. Do subscribed customers spend more than non-subscribers?  
6. Which 5 products have the highest percentage of purchases with discounts applied?  
7. How can customers be segmented into New, Returning, and Loyal based on purchase history?  
8. What are the top 3 most purchased products within each category?  
9. Are repeat buyers (more than 5 previous purchases) more likely to subscribe?  
10. What is the revenue contribution of each age group?
